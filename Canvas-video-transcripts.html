Sustainable Futures

Welcome

Speaker: Dr Helen Holmes

Hello and welcome to Sustainable Futures Foundations, an interdisciplinary training program developed by the University of Manchester. Sustainable Futures is a platform which brings together the unique depth and breadth of world-leading environmental research at the University of Manchester.

Did you know that by 2050, 1.2 billion people could be displaced globally by the impacts of climate change? That heat waves alone could cost $7.1 trillion to the global economy? And that there could be more plastic in the ocean than fish?

The multifaceted challenges of climate change, environmental pollution, biodiversity loss and just transitions require ambitious action at a pace to ensure a sustainable future for society. However, the challenges of sustainability are complex, creating both opportunities and risks. Responses to one challenge may create unintended consequences in another.

A great example of this is from my own research on waste and recycling. For a time, it was thought that the best way to deal with disposed plastic bottles was to recycle them into sportswear. Whilst this sounds like a great solution to a persistent problem, it had significant unintended consequences of its own. Sportswear is generally washed more than any other clothing type, and each time it's washed, it releases thousands of microplastic particles into the watercourse. These particles end up in the environment, posing risks to both aquatic life and human health. Hence, the most effective recycling option for plastic bottles is to recycle them back into bottles, at least for now.

At Sustainable Futures we take an interdisciplinary approach to complex challenges like these, connecting core environmental expertise and broader societal agendas to develop and test systemic, innovative and actionable solutions.

Our program is structured around six urgent and interconnected challenge areas. These are Resilience, Resources, Net-Zero, Equity, Health and Skills. These represent the broad futures which we wish to create and shape. We've organised the Sustainable Futures Foundations training around the same areas, pulling out the most interesting and uniquely challenging aspects of each and the diverse relationships that exist between them.

As a participant, you gain a key understanding required for each challenge and a reflective space to apply the learnings to your professional and personal lives. You'll be provided with a variety of exercises and opportunities to dive deeper into selected topics.

Through this training, we seek to explore the overlaps and contradictions, unpacking the messy complexity of sustainability and emphasizing the importance of interdisciplinary and critical thinking. We hope this training equips you with the knowledge, tools, and motivation to drive meaningful change in your work and beyond.
